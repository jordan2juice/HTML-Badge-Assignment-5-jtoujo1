About Us

Joe's Bar-B-Que is based in a small town of Kentucky. The restaurant goes back over 5 generations. All of the generations had added a piece of themselves to the business. The sause is a secret family recipe.

Joe, Phil, & Lou are the newest of the generation and will welcome you in with open arms.
